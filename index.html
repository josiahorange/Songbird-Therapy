Meet the Therapist

I’m Amanda, a warm, person-centred counsellor offering a calm, collaborative space where you can feel seen, heard and supported. I work with adults and young people on a number of issues, anxiety, self-esteem, relationships, grief and loss, identity, body image, spiritual concerns, and life transitions.

I have particular experience with bereavement, long-term illness, and the pressures many young people carry. If faith matters to you, I’m happy to integrate it in a way that honours your beliefs.

My approach is integrative and tailored to you. I draw from a range of approaches and tailor the work to your unique needs and pace. I recognise that challenges can stem from the past, present, or future, and I aim to meet you wherever you are. Together we’ll make sense of the past, attend to the present, and gently create space for change. Clients describe me as intuitive, grounded, and compassionate. Above all, I believe a safe, accepting relationship can foster healing, resilience, and a clearer, more confident path forward.

Why Songbird Therapy?

The name Songbird Therapy reflects the power of finding your voice - especially in times of struggle. Just as a songbird sings even in the dark, therapy can be a space to rediscover your voice, your strength, and your sense of self. Whether you're feeling silenced, overwhelmed, or unsure, this is a place where your story matters and where expression, in whatever form it takes, is gently welcomed.
